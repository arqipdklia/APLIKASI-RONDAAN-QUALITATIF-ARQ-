Privacy Policy for ARQ

Last updated: April 2, 2026

ARQ ("the App") is an internal operational application used to manage patrol, visit, checkpoint, reporting, supervision, and administrative workflows. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and shares information when authorized users access and use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on your role and how you use the App, ARQ may collect and process the following categories of information:

A. Account and Identity Information

  • Name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Account role and access level
  • Assigned formation, contingent, district, station, or branch
  • Account credentials used for internal access

B. Operational and Visit Data

  • Visit or patrol records
  • Assignment or duty type
  • Team information
  • Remarks or notes entered by users
  • Location or premise name
  • Date and time of visit
  • Visit status and related reporting data

C. Location Information

  • Precise device location
  • Approximate location
  • Coordinates used for visit validation, location registration, mapping, and supervision features

D. Device and Verification Data

  • NFC tag identifiers
  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) identifiers
  • Registered checkpoint or marker information
  • Device-related technical data needed for feature operation

E. Audio Input

  • Microphone input when the user activates voice command or speech recognition features

F. Files and Local Storage Data

  • Generated reports or PDF files saved to device storage
  • Map cache or related files stored locally for application use

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate and manage authorized user accounts
  • Assign access rights based on user role and formation
  • Verify patrol, visit, checkpoint, and supervision activities
  • Register and manage NFC, BLE, GPS, and premise or location information
  • Display maps, visit data, dashboards, summaries, and reports
  • Generate PDF or printable reports
  • Send email notifications related to visit operations
  • Support voice-command features when requested by the user
  • Maintain app security, integrity, and internal administration

3. Permissions Used by the App

  • Location: to validate visits, register locations, and support map-based functions.
  • Microphone: to enable voice command and speech recognition.
  • NFC: to read and register NFC tags for checkpoint verification.
  • Bluetooth / Bluetooth Scan / Bluetooth Connect: to detect and validate BLE devices or identifiers.
  • Storage / File Access: to generate, save, access, or export reports and operational files, and to support local map caching where applicable.
  • Internet: to connect to backend services and load operational data.

These permissions are used only for app functionality and are not intended for unrelated tracking or advertising purposes.

4. Data Sources and Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services and platform components to operate. These may include:

  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Play Services Location
  • Google Maps Platform
  • OpenStreetMap / osmdroid
  • Google Sheets API, where used to display or retrieve operational map-related data
  • Email delivery services configured by the app publisher or administrator

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With authorized administrators, supervisors, or organizational personnel who need access for operational purposes
  • With service providers and infrastructure providers that support app functionality, such as backend database, mapping, location, or email services
  • Where required to comply with applicable law, lawful request, or internal security or compliance requirements
  • To protect the security, integrity, rights, or safety of the App, the organization, or its users

6. Data Storage and Security

Data processed by ARQ may be stored in cloud services, including Firebase, and may also be stored temporarily on the user’s device for operational use, such as report generation or map cache.

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the App’s functions
  • Maintain operational records
  • Comply with organizational, legal, security, or audit requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce access controls

8. User Choices and Access

Authorized users may request updates or corrections to their account or operational information through the organization’s administrator or app publisher, subject to applicable rules and internal access controls.

Users may also control certain device permissions through Android system settings. Disabling some permissions may prevent parts of the App from functioning properly.

9. Children’s Privacy

ARQ is not intended for children and is designed for authorized operational or administrative users. We do not knowingly provide the App for use by children.

10. International Data Processing

Depending on the services used by the App, data may be processed on servers located outside the user’s immediate region. By using the App, authorized users understand that operational data may be processed through third-party infrastructure required for the App to function.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after an update means the updated Privacy Policy will apply.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the handling of data in ARQ, please contact the app publisher or organization administrator using the contact information provided in the Google Play listing or your organization’s official support channel.
